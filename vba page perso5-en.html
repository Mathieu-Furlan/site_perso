Fifth case: sort two columns alphabetically.

Summary of the problem:

There are two columns in undefined order, how to sort them alphabetically

The first one in priority over the second.

the code to fulfill the request:

	
	Sub trialpha()

	Range("A1:B9").Sort Key1:=Range("A1"), Key2:=Range("B1"), Orientation:=xlSortColumns
	Range("B1:B9").Sort Key1:=Range("B1"), Orientation:=xlSortColumns


	End Sub
