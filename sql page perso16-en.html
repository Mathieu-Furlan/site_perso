Sixteenth case: Fetch closest received dates after each requested dates.

What we have:

donneea
po requesteddate
14888 12/01/2018
14733 12/02/2018
14555 12/05/2018

donneeb
po receiveddate
14888 11/01/2018
14888 14/01/2018
14733 11/02/2018
14733 12/02/2018
14555 23/07/2018
14555 23/09/2018

Expected result:

result
po requesteddate nearestreceiveddate
14555 12/05/2018 23/07/2018
14733 12/02/2018 12/02/2018
14888 12/01/2018 14/01/2018

The query to obtain above result:

SELECT a.po, a.requesteddate, Min(receiveddate) AS nearestreceiveddate
FROM donneea AS a, donneeb AS b
WHERE a.po = b.po AND b.receiveddate >= a.requesteddate
GROUP BY a.po, a.requesteddate;
