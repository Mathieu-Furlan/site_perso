Twenty-heighth case: duplicating rows.

What we have:

donnee
acctnum cancelflag saledate saleprice
1 Y 27/06/2022 100
2 Y 10/05/2022 156
3 25/07/2022 200
4 Y 25/07/2022 150

We would like to duplicate rows which have "Y" as cancelflag.

Sale price should be multiplied by -1:

result
acctnum defcancelflag saledate defsaleprice
1 Y 27/06/2022 100
1 27/06/2022 -100
2 Y 10/05/2022 156
2 10/05/2022 -156
4 Y 25/07/2022 150
4 25/07/2022 -150

The query to achieve above result:

SELECT acctnum, cancelflag As defcancelflag, saledate, saleprice As defsaleprice
FROM donnee
WHERE cancelflag = "Y"

UNION ALL SELECT acctnum, Replace(cancelflag, "Y","") As defcancelflag, saledate, saleprice * -1 As defsaleprice
FROM donnee
WHERE cancelflag = "Y"
ORDER BY acctnum, defcancelflag DESC;
