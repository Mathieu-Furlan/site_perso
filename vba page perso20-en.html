Twentieth case: Sum if dates are matching.

Summary of the problem:

I am trying to sum values until the end of a column, if dates in columns "E" match the ones in column "A".

How to attain expected result:

	
	Sub sommesi()

	Dim i As Integer
	Dim r As Date


	For i = 1 To Cells(Rows.Count, 5).End(xlUp).Row
    
		r = Cells(i, 5)
		Cells(i, 6) = Application.SumIf(Range("A:A"), r, Range("B:B"))
    
	Next i

	End Sub
